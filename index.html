About Me

I'm Darina, a young and passionate designer motivated to create and provide solutions for better user experience in the digital world. Whether it's a branding project, a website, or a product design, I approach each challenge with creativity, resolve and an open mind.


After exploring both a BA in Communication Design and a BSc in Digital Media, I am determined to help people find their voice through technical and artistic manner alike. My desire to learn never left me so I’m always open to new ideas and business.
